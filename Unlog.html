This guide helps you access Google News (and similar services) even if login is required.

  5. Click the 3 dots next to accounts.google.com and select "Don't allow to save data"

  7. Press "Done" and reload the page
  8. Visit a page blocked by Google Admin (like Google Translate)
  9. If it says "Sign in required", close the tab
